Polygon Bridge Destinations

September 15th 2021

Introduction

Since Polygon is a sidechain off the Ethereum network, we are interested in what users are interacting with after transferring their funds from Ethereum. To do this, we can make use of Flipside Crypto's labelled data. This will allow us to get a better picture of what is attracting people to Polygon and how this has changed over time.
